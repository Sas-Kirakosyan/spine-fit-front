Safety & Injury Prevention

Essential tips for safe training, especially with back pain or injury concerns

Warning Signs

  • Stop if you feel sharp pain
  • Avoid exercises that cause numbness
  • Listen to your body's signals

Safe Practices

  • Warm up properly before exercising
  • Maintain proper form at all times
  • Start with lighter weights
  • Focus on controlled movements

Recovery

  • Allow adequate rest between sessions
  • Include stretching and mobility work
  • Consider professional guidance if needed
