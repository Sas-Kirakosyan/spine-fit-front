Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SpineFit ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use the SpineFit web and mobile applications (collectively, the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

Health data notice: SpineFit processes health-related information you provide (pain conditions, fitness level, body measurements). This data is used solely to generate your personalized workout plan and is never sold or shared with advertisers.

1. Who We Are

SpineFit is an AI-assisted spine rehabilitation and fitness tracking application created by Sas Kirakosyan. For questions about this Policy, contact us at sas.kirakosian@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you register, we collect your email address through Supabase Authentication. We do not collect your name, phone number, or payment information.

2.2 Health & Body Profile Data

To generate your personalized workout plan, we collect the following through our onboarding quiz:

  • Gender and birth year
  • Diagnosed spinal conditions: L5/S1 compression, sciatica, herniated disc
  • Current fitness level and available gym equipment
  • Desired training frequency and goals
  • General activity level
  • Height and weight (web app only — stored in your browser's localStorage, not uploaded to our servers)

Quiz answers are stored in Supabase and associated with your user ID.

2.3 Workout Logs

When you track workouts, we store exercises performed, sets/reps/weights, pain level ratings, and session timestamps. This data is stored in Supabase and used for progress tracking and progressive overload recommendations.

2.4 AI-Generated Workout Plans

Your AI-generated workout plan is stored in Supabase so it persists across sessions and devices.

2.5 Device & Preference Data

Language and theme preferences are stored in localStorage (web) or device storage (mobile) only — they are not uploaded to our servers.

2.6 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Location or GPS data
  • Contacts, photos, or camera data
  • Analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Payment information (SpineFit is free)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect exclusively to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Generate a personalized, spine-safe AI workout plan tailored to your health profile
  • Display your workout history and progress charts
  • Monitor pain-level patterns to surface training insights
  • Remember your language and theme preferences across sessions
  • Respond to support requests you initiate

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or marketing to third parties.

4. How Your Data Is Shared — Third-Party Services

4.1 Supabase

We use Supabase (Supabase, Inc.) as our authentication and database provider. Your account data, quiz answers, workout logs, and AI plans are stored on Supabase servers (US/EU). Supabase is GDPR-compliant and acts as a data processor under a Data Processing Agreement. See supabase.com/privacy.

4.2 Google Gemini AI

To generate your personalized workout plan, your quiz answers (gender, birth year, pain conditions, fitness level, equipment, training frequency, goals, and activity level) are sent to the Google Gemini 2.0 Flash API via our backend server. We do not share your email address or workout history with Google. See Google's Privacy Policy.

4.3 No Other Third-Party Sharing

We use no advertising networks, analytics services, or social login providers. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with any third party.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. When you delete your account (Settings → Danger Zone), all data associated with your account is permanently deleted from Supabase. Locally stored preferences are removed from your device when you clear your browser data or uninstall the app.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Delete your account at any time from Settings → Danger Zone, or by emailing us. Account deletion permanently removes all your data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format.
  • Objection / Restriction: Object to or request restriction of processing in certain circumstances.

To exercise any right, email sas.kirakosian@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Security

All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Supabase uses industry-standard encryption at rest. Your records are protected by Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) — only your authenticated session can read or write your data.

No method of electronic transmission is 100% secure. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

SpineFit is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in the United States and EU (via Supabase) and in Google's infrastructure (for AI plan generation). By using the Service, you consent to this transfer. Where required, we rely on Supabase's Standard Contractual Clauses for GDPR-compliant transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.

11. Medical Disclaimer

SpineFit is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. AI-generated workout plans are informational only and are not a substitute for advice from a licensed physician or physiotherapist. Always consult your doctor before starting any exercise program, especially if you have a diagnosed medical condition.
